We Develop To Simplify

Founded in September 2019, The Founders is an Information Technology company aimed at becoming the powerhouse and leading institution within the developments and innovations regarding world class Artificial Intelligence, Neural Networks and Machine Learning. We only develop solutions to simplify daily problems faced with the citizens of the world.

What We Do

The fundamental cores of our innovations may be human-mind-like functioning systems but our systems will rely on human input to give error free results, maximizing human-software efficiency

Our flag system developed will solve Africa's, possibly global issue of unclaimed human corpses at government morgues, which will relieve the governments of unplanned expenses in storing and burial of unknown deceased persons, also relieve families with grievances of their passed on loved ones immediately
